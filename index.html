FAQ

Your questions, answered

Is Rakuten UK legit?

Yes — unambiguously. Rakuten is a Japanese multinational founded in 1997, now one of the world's largest internet and e-commerce companies. They previously sponsored FC Barcelona and operate businesses in banking, fintech, digital content, and more. Rakuten UK is part of this global group. With 666+ UK Trustpilot reviews at 4.5★ and a decades-long track record, there is no legitimacy concern. Cashback is a well-established business model where retailers pay Rakuten a commission for sending them customers, and Rakuten shares that commission with you.

How does the £25 welcome bonus work exactly?

Sign up via a referral link (not directly from Rakuten's homepage — the bonus is only activated via a referral link). Then make purchases totalling £50 or more (excluding VAT) at any Rakuten partner retailers within 90 days of signing up. This can be spread across multiple purchases and multiple retailers. Once the retailer confirms the purchases — which can take up to 90 days — the £25 bonus and your cashback become payable and are paid on the next monthly payment date (around the 15th).

Why do I need a referral link to get the £25 bonus?

The £25 welcome offer is part of Rakuten's refer-a-friend scheme, not their standard sign-up bonus. Signing up directly at rakuten.co.uk without a referral link gets a smaller welcome bonus (if any). The referral link on this page activates the full £25 bonus for you — and both you and the referrer receive £25 when your qualifying spend is confirmed. It's in both parties' interest to use the referral route.

Why hasn't my cashback appeared yet?

Cashback at Rakuten typically appears in your account within a few hours of purchase, but can take up to 3 days to show as pending. It then stays as pending until the retailer confirms the sale — this takes 60–90 days for most retailers (because they wait until their return window closes). Fast-clearing retailers like Just Eat typically confirm within days. If cashback hasn't shown as pending within 48 hours, use Rakuten's "Missing Cash Back" feature — multiple users confirm instant credit in most cases.

Does Rakuten work for in-store shopping?

Yes. Rakuten offers in-store cashback at participating UK retailers. To activate it, you link your credit or debit card to your Rakuten account, then activate specific in-store offers for the retailers you plan to visit. When you pay with your linked card in-store at that retailer, cashback is automatically credited. This works alongside your regular card rewards — just note that you must activate each in-store offer individually before shopping.

Can I stack Rakuten cashback with my credit card rewards?

Yes — this is one of Rakuten's best-kept features. Rakuten cashback and credit card rewards are completely separate and stack fully. If you use an American Express card earning Membership Rewards points, you earn both the Amex points and the Rakuten cashback on the same purchase. For Sainsbury's at 10.5% Rakuten cashback plus 1–2% Amex points, you're effectively earning 11–13% back on your weekly shop — entirely passively.

How does Rakuten compare to TopCashback and Quidco?

Honest answer: TopCashback and Quidco typically offer more retailers and sometimes higher cashback rates at specific merchants. Rakuten's key advantages are: Amazon cashback (TopCashback no longer offers this reliably), the £25 referral bonus (much more generous than TopCashback's £10), automatic monthly PayPal payment (no manual withdrawal needed), and strong performance at grocers like Sainsbury's. Many UK users run multiple cashback sites simultaneously, using whichever offers the best rate for any given purchase — and Rakuten is worth having for the retailers where it leads.

What happens if my cashback doesn't track correctly?

The most common cause of missing cashback is using a VPN, ad blocker, or another browser extension (including other cashback tools) simultaneously. Disable these before completing a Rakuten-tracked purchase. If cashback still doesn't appear within 48 hours, raise a "Missing Cash Back" claim from your Rakuten account — multiple reviews confirm this is resolved quickly with credit issued almost immediately in most cases. Rakuten's customer support is consistently praised in UK reviews for responsiveness.
